About PillShare

We collect unused, safe medicines and redistribute them to the poor and needy through verified partners and community networks.

How It Works

  1. Donors fill an online form to share surplus medicines.
  2. Volunteers collect the medicines and verify expiry and condition.
  3. We distribute them to NGOs, clinics, and individuals in need.
